# 14. Templates Index

Use these templates as operational records. They can live in Markdown, a notebook, cards, a wiki, a database, or a future app.

## Template map

### `templates/world_kernel.md`

Use once at world creation and revise deliberately.

Defines:

- essence;
- aesthetic promise;
- primary pressures;
- starting scale;
- forbidden easy answers;
- mystery posture;
- initial seeds.

### `templates/canon_fact_card.md`

Use for every atomic canon fact.

Defines:

- fact;
- truth layer;
- status;
- dependencies;
- affected domains;
- consequences;
- contradictions;
- decision.

### `templates/capability_card.md`

Use for abilities, technologies, magic, rituals, weapons, institutions, resources, or laws that let actors produce repeatable effects.

Defines:

- effect;
- access;
- cost;
- risk;
- countermeasures;
- diffusion;
- institutional response;
- economic impact.

### `templates/institution_card.md`

Use for guilds, states, temples, courts, families, markets, clans, cults, militaries, schools, and informal customs.

Defines:

- problem solved;
- rules;
- rules-in-use;
- enforcement;
- legitimacy;
- beneficiaries;
- failure modes.

### `templates/action_arena_card.md`

Use for recurring choice situations.

Defines:

- participants;
- actions;
- information;
- rules;
- payoffs;
- likely outcomes;
- feedback.

### `templates/propagation_report.md`

Use after admitting or revising a major fact.

Defines:

- orders of consequence;
- domain sweep;
- institutions;
- economy;
- daily life;
- history;
- contradictions;
- branches.

### `templates/contradiction_report.md`

Use for conflicts.

Defines:

- conflict type;
- affected facts;
- severity;
- repair options;
- decision.

### `templates/mystery_ledger_entry.md`

Use to preserve unknowns.

Defines:

- known effects;
- unknown essence;
- competing interpretations;
- forbidden explanations;
- reveal conditions.

### `templates/canon_change_proposal.md`

Use for human or AI-generated proposals before canonization.

Defines:

- proposed change;
- analysis;
- branch options;
- recommendation;
- human decision.

## Template principle

Templates are not paperwork. They are thinking traps designed to catch shallow canon before it damages the world.
