# Counter-Institution Card

Use for underground clinics, rebel courts, black markets, smuggling guilds, heretical schools, mutual-aid networks, secret archives, outlaw temples, pirate radio, and unofficial law.

## Counter-institution

Name in plain language.

## Official institution opposed or bypassed

## Reason for existence

Exclusion, cost, corruption, taboo, suppression, survival, identity, efficiency, fraud, rebellion, sacred duty.

## Users

## Services or rules

What does it provide, replace, distort, or enforce?

## Trust mechanism

How do participants know it will not betray them?

## Enforcement

## Risks

State violence, scams, impurity, contagion, betrayal, faction capture, moral compromise.

## Economy

Prices, barter, favors, protection, debt, secrecy costs.

## Suppression and adaptation
