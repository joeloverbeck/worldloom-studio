# Uncertainty / Evidence Card

## Truth layer

## Confidence band

Observation, motive, access, distortion, copying, translation, coercion, expertise.

## Belief communities

## Standards of proof

## Evidence habitat

What institutions, rituals, records, jokes, songs, offices, archives, or punishments keep the belief alive?

## What would change confidence

New witness, artifact, ritual, experiment, confession, map, body, fossil, price movement, omen, contradiction.
