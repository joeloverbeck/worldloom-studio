# Mystery / Wonder Ledger Entry

A mystery / wonder ledger entry records a protected unknown or protected non-instrumental effect. It is a thinking instrument, not a software record. Use it for governed mysteries, wonder/awe/sublimity, sacred opacity, horror/terror/dread, symbolic excess, or hybrids.

## Protected effect type

Governed mystery / wonder-awe-sublimity / sacred opacity / horror-terror-dread / symbolic excess / hybrid.

## Puzzle question, if any

Write the question if this is a mystery. Write `none` if there is no puzzle and the protected effect is wonder, sacred opacity, horror, dread, or symbolic excess.

## Mystery type, if applicable

Epistemic / metaphysical / moral / historical / identity / motive / system / authorial mystery boundary / not applicable.

## What is fixed

What is true regardless of what characters know?

## What is author-secret

What the steward knows but characters or audience may not know yet.

## What is deliberately undecided

What is intentionally not settled because undecidability is part of the design.

## What is forbidden

What answer, explanation, use, measurement, mapping, price, reveal, or adaptation must not be introduced because it would damage the protected effect.

## Truth-layer boundaries

Separate objective canon, author-secret canon, branch canon, diegetic claim, public belief, local belief, elite belief, mythic truth, propaganda, lie, honest error, disputed claim, and mystery boundary.

## Preservation boundary

What must remain awe-filled, sacred, dreadful, excessive, ambiguous, or larger than explanation?

## Who knows partial truth

Characters, institutions, communities, gods, monsters, archives, unreliable witnesses, or audiences.

## False beliefs

What wrong theories circulate, and what consequences do they create?

## Evidence available

Clues, residues, relics, testimonies, scars, patterns, songs, dreams, failed experiments, or sensory signals.

## Evidence missing

What cannot yet be known, found, measured, remembered, decoded, or safely spoken?

## Evidence habitat

Where evidence lives: archive, body, ruin, ritual, gossip, dream, landscape, monster wound, court record, sacred silence, recurring image, game mechanic, or medium-specific sign.

## Consequences already visible

Institutions, rituals, prices, taboos, fears, rumors, cults, frauds, jokes, pilgrimages, avoidance habits, songs, motifs, dread patterns, sacred architecture, or ordinary-life residue.

## Recurrence / motif / transformation

How the protected effect repeats or changes form without being solved away.

## Reveal permissions

What may be revealed, to whom, when, in which branch or medium, and at what cost?

## Reveal prohibitions

What must not be revealed or converted into a puzzle answer?

## Explanation-pressure operation

Reveal / delay / forbid / consecrate / dread-preserve / excess-preserve / translate.

## What would break if solved or flattened

Name emotional, symbolic, sacred, horror, continuity, gameplay, or thematic damage.

## Preservation checks

- Consequences remain visible.
- Fixed, secret, undecided, and forbidden material are separated.
- No protected effect is used to hide an ordinary contradiction.
- Wonder, sacredness, horror, or symbolic excess is not converted into a puzzle unless that is the deliberate branch.
- A reveal, if allowed, creates new consequence rather than merely closing a file.

## Traceability note

Parent protocol: `13_contradiction_retcon_and_mystery.md`.

This card is a human reasoning instrument, not a database schema or implementation contract. Use it to protect meaning, awe, dread, and governed unknowns without confusing them with canon status, constraint tags, decision operations, or repair operations.

## Minimum completion

- State the affected fact, scope, branch, or world section.
- Record the decision-relevant pressure, not every possible detail.
- Name unresolved canon debt if the card exposes it.
- Cross-reference any parent protocol, checklist, contradiction report, preservation sweep, branch diff, or decision record needed to keep the change governed.


## Sacred-opacity accountability (required if effect type includes sacred opacity)

- **Refused explanation:** What exact origin, essence, or ultimate meaning is protected?
- **Consequences still fixed:** What costs, residues, ritual conditions, witness effects, spatial limits, or temporal limits remain auditable?
- **Recurrence / boundary rule:** What pattern distinguishes an earned exception from an arbitrary authorial convenience?
- **Contradiction implications:** What existing or future canon facts would need repair if they violate this boundary?
- **Critique still allowed:** Which craft questions remain fair: clarity, recurrence, tone, cost, evidence, audience trust, or aesthetic fit?
- **Legitimate-vs-cheat note:** State why this is earned sacred opacity rather than a sacred-opacity cheat.

> Micro-example: “The chapel mouth's origin is protected; its noon/sunset speech window, named-person rule, shadow cost, candle-voice profanation, and witness disputes remain governed.”
