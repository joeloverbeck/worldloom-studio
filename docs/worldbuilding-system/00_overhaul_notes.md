## Structural summary of iteration three

Iteration three keeps the iteration-two 24-document spine, 12 checklists, and 18 templates. Consolidation by deletion was rejected because the current set already had useful tier separation and no file was redundant enough to remove without risking loss of valid craft coverage.

The iteration-three work therefore converges rather than rebuilds:

- preserves the consequence-first constitution, the 12 operating laws, the 13 truth-layer order, shock-cone propagation, and the canon-status / decision-operation / repair-operation separation;
- wires every checklist to the protocol it operationalizes, including the seven previously orphaned checklists named in the brief;
- also wires `checklists/propagation_sweep.md`, `checklists/institutional_economic_suppression_sweep.md`, and `checklists/mystery_preservation.md` more directly to their parent protocols;
- reconciles admission decision operations and contradiction repair operations through `03`, `13`, and the authoritative glossary;
- clarifies that constraint tags coexist with canon statuses rather than replacing them;
- standardizes `black market` vocabulary and removes the stray `shadow markets` usage;
- expands the glossary with missing load-bearing terms: `black market`, `cost`, `latency`, `operation`, `path dependence`, `residue`, `rules-in-use`, and `transaction cost`;
- assigns mystery coverage: doctrine in `01`, repair/mystery ledger in `13`, evidence and belief ecology in `14`;
- updates templates so status, tags, admission decisions, and repair operations do not blur;
- updates `manifest.json` to version `0.3`.
