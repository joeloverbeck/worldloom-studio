## Structural summary

This overhaul preserves the original consequence-first doctrine and expands the package from a 16-document spine into a 23-document spine plus expanded templates and checklists.

The main structural changes are:

- `08_constraint_composition.md` added to make limits, bottlenecks, conflicts, loopholes, and chains explicit.
- `09_temporal_and_timeline_protocol.md` added for causality order, latency, eras, fossils, retcons, and branch chronology.
- `10_spatial_and_geographic_propagation.md` added for terrain, settlement, routes, barriers, and regional diffusion.
- `11_agent_character_psychology.md` added for individual agency, bounded knowledge, identity, incentives, and character pressure.
- Original institutional and economic analysis expanded into `12_institutional_economic_and_suppression_protocol.md`, adding counter-institutions and suppression politics.

- Original narrative/game extraction expanded into `16_narrative_game_and_transmedia_extraction.md`.
- `17_aesthetic_coherence_and_semiosis.md` added to deepen tone, language, symbol, naming, and sensory residue.
- QA, examples, AI workflow, templates, glossary, bibliography, README, and manifest were regenerated to match the new structure.

## Drift reconciliation

### Canon status, decisions, and repairs

The old package mixed canon-status labels, admission decisions, and repair operations. The overhaul separates them:

- **Canon status** records standing: proposed, under review, accepted, accepted with constraints, localized, contested, quarantined, branch-only, superseded, deprecated, rejected.
- **Decision operation** records admission action: accept, constrain, localize, historicize, reinterpret, institutionalize, price, branch, quarantine, supersede, deprecate, reject.
- **Repair operation** records contradiction treatment: clarify scope, add constraint, price, localize, historicize, institutionalize, diffuse unevenly, reinterpret, split, retcon, quarantine, reject.

No meaningful distinction was removed. The old ideas survive under clearer categories.

### “Accepted as X” variants

The old “accepted as local/rare/recent/secret/misunderstood” variants are now **constraint tags** attached to accepted facts rather than separate statuses. This preserves meaning while removing status proliferation.

### Truth-layer order

The canonical truth-layer order is now:

1. objective canon;
2. author-secret canon;
3. branch canon;
4. mystery boundary;
5. diegetic claim;
6. public belief;
7. local belief;
8. elite belief;
9. mythic truth;
10. propaganda;
11. lie;
12. honest error;
13. disputed claim.

### Shock cone

The glossary term “shock cone” is retained and adopted throughout propagation docs. It names the widening pattern of consequence from a canon fact.

## Research-driven rationale

- Systems-thinking expansion and leverage-point language informed the deeper consequence and feedback treatment.
- Ostrom, North, and Williamson informed the strengthened institution/economy/action-arena protocol.
- Rogers, Tobler, and Hägerstrand informed the new spatial diffusion protocol.
- Simon, Ajzen, and Tajfel/Turner informed the new agent psychology protocol.
- Belief revision and Bayesian epistemology informed the uncertainty/evidence protocol.
- Peirce, Hymes, and sociolinguistic variation informed the aesthetic/semiotic protocol.
- MDA informed the game repeatability and mechanics-as-world-permission sections.
- Scott informed the suppression and counter-institution material.

All external research is kept in the research lane and is not used to assert target-repository state.
