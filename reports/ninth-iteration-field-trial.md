## Verdict

**Version 0.8 works, and the eighth iteration's repairs specifically work.** A steward following the path literally built a second small world from nothing to a QA-passed stable candidate with no off-path reads, no improvised instruments (one improvised *container*, Q2-01), and no structural friction. **Convergence, against the outlook's own gate (§2):** the outlook defined the ripeness condition as "a second field trial, run cold on 0.8, whose friction log demands no structural change and confirms the 0.8 additions carry their weight." Both clauses are met. No F2/Q2 item touches `01`–`04`; a ninth pass applying only this log's fixes would leave doctrine byte-identical, satisfying criterion 1.
