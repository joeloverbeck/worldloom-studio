## Consequence audit (highlights per seed; full ripples owed at propagation)

- **What must already be true (S2a):** a college of Office astronomers ("Intercalators") with instruments, succession, and oath discipline; a legal machinery for closing courts/registries on no notice; bell-tower command over the whole city. All admitted as dependencies at the gate.
- **Latency / why is the world not already different — the inverting question.** Year is ~300; the Rest system has run ~256 years: roughly 64 Rests plus the Still Season's nine days ≈ **73 unnumbered days already lived**. The world must be shown *saturated*, not adapting: two-age bookkeeping is ancient habit; Rest etiquette is manners, not policy; the day-broker trade has guild-like maturity, fee customs, and its own cant; the theology of the Founders' Rest is orthodoxy with schisms, not a fresh cover story. This inverts the propagation burden into a shopping list of mature residue (as in trial 1's V-05 — the checklist question does the same work here).
- **Secrecy mechanics (per `03` governance rule 3), the audit's sharpest catch:** the arithmetic is *not secret abroad*. Any foreign astronomer can count. Therefore the concealment survives domestically **not because nobody knows but because nobody with power profits from saying it aloud**: foreign chanceries bank the leverage, conversion clerks bill for the ambiguity, and the exiled sun-counters' pamphlets are unreadable inside a city where reading them is sedition. The Censors' true job is not hiding a fact but keeping public assertion unaffordable. This must be admitted explicitly (folds into S3c/S4a), or the whole premise fails the "why not already exposed" test.
- **Enemies, frauds, counter-adaptation:** forged Office stamps on contraband almanacs; false day-brokers who sell "Rest" appointments on numbered days to the gullible; provincial towns that miss the silence signal and desynchronize (spatial debt, named below).
- **Ordinary-life residue:** bell-age vs. sun-age; blank bound pages in ledgers; rent and interest customs ("no coin breeds in the quiet"); prisoners serve Rests but sentences don't count them — convicts speak of "serving the quiet"; children lawfully truant on days that don't exist.
- **Historical fossils:** the Still Season's plague story (public memory) vs. quarantine records that mention no bodies (archival anomaly the Office cannot fully scrub); pre-Rest tide-tables in fishermen's families that "run wrong" against the civil date.
- **Mysteries protected:** S1c (Founders' motive — undecided) and S5a (Toll cause — sacred opacity). Neither is being used to avoid consequence: the Toll's *regularities* are canon and owe full consequence (observation attempts, theology, Rest-detection via noon vigils — note: anyone keeping solar noon vigil can detect a Rest independently of the bells' silence; this is a real leak channel the Office must live with).
- **What changes immediately** is the wrong tense for every seed except none — all seeds are old. Immediate-change analysis applies only to the trial's later repairs.
