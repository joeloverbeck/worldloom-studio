# Propagation Report — S2a: The intercalation of the Founders' Rest

Per `templates/propagation_report.md` + `checklists/propagation_sweep.md` (boxes worked here; the report is the master shock-cone record; fact card 3 digests it). **Audit trail: not edited after the pass.**

## Fact

Since Year 44, the Office of the Kalends intercalates one unnumbered Rest roughly every four years, keeping civil dates within a day of the sun; Rests are public and ritualized; schedule and arithmetic are Office secrets.

## Severity (work scale)

Foundational (admission Level 4 per `06`'s map).

## Consequence mode

Material/institutional primary; sacred secondary.

## Initial scope

Time: Year 44–present (~64 Rests + the Still Season). Place: Carillon and bell-governed hinterland. Population: all. Branch: baseline.

## Shock-cone order

### Immediate effects

The wrong tense for a 256-year-old practice — the world is *saturated*, so "immediate" effects are read as the permanent steady state: four times a decade the city loses a lawful day; everyone within earshot organizes life around a silence they cannot predict.

### Prerequisites exposed

The Intercalator college; bell-tower command (Ringers' Guild subordination to the Office — Merla's guild is legally an Office organ, a fact she was never told in those words); closing-law machinery (S2b); the theology (S2e); the Still Season having cleared the arrears (Card 4).

### Domain ripples (14-domain pass; triage: direct = 7, 11, 9; dependency = 5, 8; reaction = 4, 6, 10, 12, 13, 14; quiet = 1 except the Toll, 2, 3 with one exception)

1. **Physics/metaphysics:** deliberately quiet — the drift is arithmetic. The one metaphysical resident (the Toll) is governed by the mystery ledger, not this report.
2. **Geography/infrastructure:** quiet at city scale; the bells' earshot boundary is the *jurisdictional* boundary in practice — named debt D-3 (hinterland sync), scoped out.
3. **Ecology/food:** one loud exception found by the sweep — **animals do not observe Rests.** Cows must be milked, bread must prove, fevers must break. The law therefore contains a lawful-labor class: **sun-work** (husbandry, baking, medicine, midwifery, watch duty), work the sun commands and the Rest cannot forbid. Folk saying: "the beasts keep the sun's count." Sun-work is the seam through which ordinary life survives the dead day — and midwifery being sun-work is precisely what makes D-1 (births on Rests) unavoidable rather than rare.
4. **Population/household:** Rest-conceived children, Rest deaths and births — **negative-domain finding: registry and inheritance law were unchanged by my seed set while demography is plainly affected. Contradiction candidate, formally flagged** (this is audit debt D-1 with its fuse now visible; assigned to the institutional pass, where the Registry arena will force it). Household custom: families keep private day-tallies (sun-age vs bell-age); elders who remember many Rests are the household's living count.
5. **Production/labor:** a day's lost wages four times a decade, priced into annual contracts ("the year of days and the year of wages differ"); apprenticeship terms specify which count they run on — a standing source of petty disputes the guilds arbitrate.
6. **Economy/trade:** eve-and-morrow settlement custom (business clusters just before and after an anticipated Rest); Rest-eve price bumps (bread, lamp oil, physicians' retainers); insurers price Rest-window risk; the **leak market** — schedule foreknowledge as a tradable good ("calendar touts") — the black-market branch's main artery, assigned to pass `12`; count-conversion trade at the ports (S4d).
7. **Governance/law:** the closing law's whole apparatus; the undated-acts law (Card 5) as the system's legal shadow; almanac licensing (S4b); the sedition boundary (Card 6). The state's legibility project (records, dates, censuses) contains a deliberate recurring hole — Carillon is a state that *chooses* four days a decade of illegibility as the price of its founding lie.
8. **War/security:** **sweep catch — a city silent on a secret schedule is a military target.** If an enemy learned the schedule, they would attack on a Rest: courts closed, no dated orders, muster confusion. Answers: watch duty is sun-work (the garrison stands on Rests); the schedule is therefore a *military* secret as well as doctrinal one — schedule espionage is the foreign intelligence problem, tying the leak market to treason and raising the Censorate's stake in the touts. (Instrument-caused insight: the "why not used by enemies" test produced the world's espionage layer.)
9. **Religion/ritual:** the Founders' Rest liturgy (forms descend from Still Season improvisations); the Temple as sincere junior partner; heterodox noon-vigil piety as devotional cover for drift-detection; the Toll's seal doctrine (S2e); a minor heresy calendar: sects that argue the Rests should be *kept holy in advance* — i.e., predicted — putting pious pressure on the very secrecy the Office needs.
10. **Culture/language:** "in the quiet" (during a Rest, with its don't-ask etiquette); "born of the morrow"; "the quiet has its pages" (blank bound ledger leaves); coin-turning after the Toll; children's knock-and-hide games; the pitying/knowing shrug that answers any question about a Rest.
11. **Knowledge/records:** licensed astronomy with forbidden conclusions; almanac stamp office; archive access licensing (guards D-4, the Still Season anomaly); schooling that teaches calendar reverence and avoids long-period arithmetic; the college's sealed noon-ledgers.
12. **History/path dependence:** the Still Season as trauma anchor (Card 4); the adjacency prohibition (no two consecutive Rests) as scar tissue from it; the constitutional gap — no organ audits the college ("who intercalates the Intercalators?") — an old emergency arrangement fossilized into a standing danger.
13. **Daily life:** two ages; private tallies; Rest-eve larders; the held breath at noon; unlit lamps; landlords' rent-clause folklore ("no coin breeds in the quiet" — interest does not accrue on Rests, a small pro-debtor mercy that makes the populace quietly fond of Rests).
14. **Aesthetics/tone:** silence as the sound of state power; bronze and ink palette confirmed; the world's identity deepens — the fact makes the Republic *more* itself (institutional dread with a sacred seam), no genre-contract strain.
