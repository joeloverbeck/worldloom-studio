# Constraint Composition — The Cellar-Bank Pressure

Per `08_constraint_composition.md` + `checklists/constraint_composition_sweep.md`. Trigger: propagation report SF-1, domain 6 flag. Multiple limits govern one fact (memory-salt) and their *composition* creates a pressure none creates alone.

## Constraint cards (compact, per `templates/constraint_card.md`)

### CC-1 — Dry durability

- **Constrained fact:** sealed grains (S1f).
- **Constraint statement:** a sealed grain keeps indefinitely if dry; any moisture ruins it irrecoverably.
- **Type:** material/environmental. **Why it exists:** metaphysical property of Shelf salt (cause inside mystery boundary — accepted per `08` "cause or accepted mystery boundary").
- **Prevents:** memory rot as default; forces active custody rather than passive keeping.
- **Enforces:** physics (humid climate is the enforcer — Saltmarrow air wants your grains dead).
- **Bypass attempts:** vault-wrights sell dryness; the poor use tallow-sealed jars (partial, riskier).
- **Loopholes:** none against moisture itself; the loophole space is all in *custody arrangements*.
- **Composition:** chains into CC-2 and the banking pressure below.
- **Residue:** cellar architecture, humidity charms, insurance exclusions for flood parishes, "drowning the cellar" as the local atrocity.

### CC-2 — Verification destroys the asset

- **Constrained fact:** grain contents (S1d/e).
- **Constraint statement:** the only way to know what a grain holds is to consume it; no reading, no copying.
- **Type:** knowledge/epistemic. **Why:** metaphysical (same boundary).
- **Prevents:** memory as media; archives of the dead; the memory-tech drift the kernel forbade.
- **Enforces:** physics/metaphysics; socially reinforced by salt-whisperer fraud prosecutions.
- **Bypass attempts:** salt-whisperers (fraud), provenance paperwork (the honest substitute: you trust the *registry*, not the grain).
- **Loopholes:** none real; the workaround economy is entirely reputational.
- **Composition:** with CC-1 + scarcity + registry, produces the trust economy below.
- **Residue:** the phrase "a grain is a promise"; probate rituals of reading the *paper* aloud before tasting.

## The composition (sweep findings)

- **Stacking:** durability (CC-1) + one-in-twenty scarcity + certification registry (S2a) stack into *store-of-value* behavior: grains are the country's most durable, most legible, least verifiable asset.
- **Gate:** Assay certification gates lawful transfer — so any grain-finance must pass through or corrupt the Assay.
- **Tradeoff:** CC-2 makes every grain an unverifiable trust object; value rides on paper and stamp, not contents. **The assay stamp is therefore a fiduciary token, and stamp forgery is systemic risk, not petty crime.**
- **Threshold:** concentration — a cellar holding many families' grains crosses from household to target (war booty, sabotage, extortion leverage). Insurers refuse cover above a headcount of diers; that exclusion line effectively *defines* what a "bank" is in law.
- **Sequential failure:** custody chain (seal → certify → register → store → transfer-tax → taste) fails worst at storage; a wet vault destroys value that paper still claims exists — **paper and salt can diverge**, the classic bank-run shape.
- **Cancellation/conflict:** S2c (transfer tax) vs. deposit banking: is a deposit a transfer? The world resolves it as a **legal fiction** (`08` conflict list): "custody without conveyance" — banks hold but do not own, so no tax; the state answers with custody-duty licensing. Recorded as institutional compromise, not contradiction.
- **Contradiction test:** composition touches C-1 (pre-war salts): may an unsellable pre-war salt be *collateral*? If yes, **foreclosure becomes a laundering channel** — the loophole through which "illegal to sell" leaks. Fed into the C-1 contradiction repair (stage 6) as a constraint-leakage input.

## Constraint stress test (five hostile questions)

1. **Rich actor:** buys custody-duty licenses and salt-whisperer prosecutions alike — owns the trust infrastructure rather than bypassing it.
2. **Desperate actor:** pawns the family grain; default is the engine of the world's bitterest small tragedies — by design.
3. **Monopolizer:** the Assay itself is the risk: it certifies, audits banks, and appoints Single Witnesses. Watch-flag recorded: Assay overreach is the world's most plausible future war. (Assigned as open pressure line, not debt.)
4. **Child/servant/exile discovers:** servants know which vaults leak; children's game "Last Words" makes them the best formula-witnesses; exiles (Lee Shore) know foreclosure laundering first.
5. **Common loophole → profession:** custody-without-conveyance spawns *deposit brokers*; the taboo it spawns: tasting a grain you merely custody is the profession's unforgivable sin, worse than theft in guild law.
