## Audit-debt resolution (from frontloaded seed audit)

**Harvest-volume gap — resolved by `constrain` + `price` during SF-1 admission:** the Shelf's crust regrows slowly; licensed neap-tide cutting yields enough grains for roughly **one death in twenty**. Sealing is therefore a minority, prestige-inflected practice; most people die unsalted. New consequences admitted with it: grief inequality, pauper's-grain charities, sects that praise dying "dry."

**Lee Shore holdings extent — deliberately kept as evidence debt,** reclassified as an in-world disputed claim (S3b). Assigned follow-up: uncertainty/evidence pass if the dispute becomes load-bearing.
